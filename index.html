Water Distribution Network

IIT Madras

The water distribution network have no proper monitoring or logging facility to keeptrack of the water levels,usage,operating time and power consumption. The manual logging takes lot of time in predicting/analysing the water consumption over different parts of the institute.In order to solve this problem an android application is being developed to monitor or log the water consumption or levels at various over-head tanks. The data collected is saved as a log file locally and will be sent to the server for further analysis.
